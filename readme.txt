Estimate your monthly mortgage payments easily. Our free Mortgage Payment Calculator factors in extra payments, taxes, and insurance to help you save money.

[https://www.calculator.io/mortgage-payment-calculator/](https://www.calculator.io/mortgage-payment-calculator/)

== Usage ==

Add the Mortgage Payment Calculator shortcode to your page, post or sidebar:

`[calcio_mortgage_payment_calculator]`

Add the following code to your template where you would like the Mortgage Payment Calculator to appear:

`<?php display_calcio_mortgage_payment_calculator(); ?>`

1. Upload the Mortgage Payment Calculator /calcio_mortgage_payment_calculator/ folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
2. Activate the Mortgage Payment Calculator plugin through the "Plugins" menu in WordPress.
